What are Atoms?
  • Typography
  • Icons
Atoms are the very basic elements of the stylesheet. Imagine every single- class element or selector rules as an atom. Examples are buttons, links, headlines, inputs ... you get it, right?
To mark a variable or mixin as an atom, annotate it with the @atom tag, followed by the name of the component. Special atoms are @icons.

This is the generated style guide for Nucleus and it's documentation pages. These are the actual components used, and the pages are generated with Nucleus itself. Feel free to browse a bit and try things out.
